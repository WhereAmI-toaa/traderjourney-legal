Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 7, 2026

TL;DR: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, sell, or track you. If you use AI features, your trades go to OpenAI (with your permission) to generate insights—OpenAI doesn't train on this data.

1. Overview

TraderJourney ("we," "our," or "the App") is a macOS trading journal application designed to help you track, analyze, and improve your trading performance. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

2. What Data We Collect

On Your Device (SwiftData):

  • Trade records (entry/exit prices, symbols, P&L, dates, durations)
  • Trade executions and details
  • Journal entries and notes
  • Screenshots and attachments
  • Tags and categories
  • Import templates
  • Daily performance statistics

From Apple Sign-In:

  • Apple User ID (stored locally only)
  • Email address (if you provide it, stored locally only)
  • Identity token (for authentication, stored in Keychain)

Important: All this data is stored only on your device. We have no server. We cannot see, access, or retrieve your data.

3. AI Assistant Data Sharing

You control this completely.

When you use the AI Trading Coach feature, the App sends trade data to OpenAI to generate insights:

  • Trade symbols, P&L, entry/exit times, strategies
  • Aggregated statistics (win rate, drawdown, etc.)

You must explicitly opt-in in Settings → Privacy & AI Data before this happens.

Important OpenAI Facts:

  • OpenAI does not train on data sent via API
  • See OpenAI's privacy policy: openai.com/enterprise-privacy
  • Data is processed to generate your insights, then discarded per your request
  • You can revoke consent anytime in Settings

4. What We Don't Do

  • ✗ No tracking, analytics, or crash reporting
  • ✗ No cookies, fingerprinting, or device identification
  • ✗ No advertising or data brokers
  • ✗ No third-party SDKs collecting your information
  • ✗ No cloud sync or backup (unless you choose to)
  • ✗ No user accounts on our servers

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

You have full control:

Delete All Data

Settings → Danger Zone → Reset Everything
Permanently deletes all trades, entries, tags, and settings. Cannot be undone.

Export Your Data

Settings → Data Management → Export Trades as CSV
Download all your trades in open format.

Revoke AI Consent

Settings → Privacy & AI Data → Toggle Off
AI Assistant becomes unavailable. Previously sent data cannot be recalled (OpenAI's policy).

Backup & Restore

Settings → Data Management → Backup / Restore
Create full database backups to external media.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used this App, all data can be permanently deleted via Settings → Reset Everything.

7. Apple Sign-In Privacy

We use Apple Sign-In for authentication. Apple may provide us with:

  • A unique user identifier
  • Your email address (if you share it)
  • Your name (if you share it)

This information is stored only on your device in the Keychain. We do not have a backend server, so this data never leaves your Mac.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. We will notify you by updating the "Effective Date" above. Your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
